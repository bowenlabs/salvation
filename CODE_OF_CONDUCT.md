# Code of Conduct & Acceptable Use

This document covers two things: how people treat each other in this
project, and what Citadel may be used to build.

---

## Community standards

All contributions are welcome, regardless of race, ethnicity, national
origin, religion, sex, gender identity or expression, sexual orientation,
age, disability, or veteran status. Civil rights are non-negotiable here.

Examples of unacceptable behaviour in issues, PRs, discussions, or any
other project space:

- Hateful or discriminatory language or imagery, in any form
- Harassment, bullying, or personal attacks — public or private
- Sexualized language or imagery, or unwelcome advances
- Doxxing or sharing others' private information without consent
- Sustained disruption of discussions or deliberate bad-faith arguments

Disagreement about code, architecture, or direction is fine and expected.
Attacking the person instead of the idea is not.

### Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues/PRs, or ban contributors
who violate this policy, at their discretion and without warning for
severe violations. Report concerns to the maintainers via a private
channel (not a public issue) — see [README.md](README.md) for contact.

---

## Acceptable use of Citadel

Citadel is free, and we want it to stay easy to self-host without
gatekeeping. That said, it may not be used to build or operate sites
that:

- Promote hatred, violence, or discrimination against any group on the
  basis of race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation,
  disability, or similar protected characteristics
- Harass, bully, stalk, or incite harm against individuals or groups
- Exist primarily to spread the above, even if individual pages look
  innocuous

This is about how Citadel is used, not about who can use it — the project
itself remains open to all contributors and operators in good faith.
